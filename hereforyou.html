Stripping Restrictive Covenants

Many historic property deeds throughout King and Snohomish counties still contain offensive, illegal language explicitly banning marginalized groups from owning or occupying the home.

A verified excerpt from an original residential property title record in King County, WA.

Transparent Financial Programs

No vague qualifying parameters. Below are the precise, verified baseline criteria and actual income caps for localized purchase assistance programs across King and Snohomish Counties.

The Washington Covenant Homeownership Program

DOWN PAYMENT & CLOSING GRANT

A landmark zero-interest program providing up to 20% of the purchase price (capped at $150,000) to explicitly assist first-time homebuyers harmed by historic discriminatory housing covenants.

  • Exact Income Limits: Household income must not exceed **$197,300** annually for King and Snohomish counties.
  • Core Rule: Must be a first-time homebuyer who resided in Washington State prior to April 11, 1968 (or be a direct descendant).
  • Repayment Term: 0% interest loan. Zero monthly payments. Principal balance is only due when you sell, transfer, or fully pay off the primary mortgage.

The Sam Smith "Hi Neighbor" Homeownership Fund

PURCHASING POWER BOOST

Managed via HomeSight, this fund provides up to **$12,000** in layered down payment assistance specifically designed to bridge the homeownership gap facing Black and African American buyers within Washington State.

  • Exact Income Limits: Specifically targets buyers earning between 80% and 120% of the Area Median Income. In King County, this translates strictly to a qualifying threshold between **$116,650** and **$174,970** depending on precise family sizing metrics.
  • Core Rule: Must complete an approved homebuyer education class and occupy the property as a primary residence.

WSHFC Home Advantage Assistance

STATE SPONSORED DPA LOAN

Standard statewide second-mortgage program providing up to 4% or 5% of your total loan value to explicitly clear upfront out-of-pocket cash requirements at close.

  • Exact Income Limits: Total global household income cannot exceed **$180,000** annually for both King and Snohomish counties.
  • Core Rule: Requires a minimum credit score of 620 and utilizing a state-authorized, commission-trained lending partner.
