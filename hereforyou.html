Historical Deed Tracking

Decades ago, developers across King and Snohomish Counties utilized discriminatory language inside neighborhood plat maps and property deeds—known as racial restrictive covenants—to explicitly bar minority families from buying homes. While these entries were ruled completely illegal and void by state and federal laws decades ago, the text often remains buried deep within historical land titles.

My Offer to the Community

You should never have to look at your home's deed and see remnants of discrimination. If you live in our local area and your home's historic chain of title contains a legacy restrictive covenant, I will handle the research and modification tracking completely free of charge:
