About Me

I transplanted to Seattle in 2009, and the Pacific Northwest has become my new home. I am in love with its natural beauty and the abundance of year-round activities. Having grown up around the construction business, I developed a deep understanding of what makes a good home. My real estate passion comes from my desire to help you navigate the journey of purchasing one of the largest yet most personal items of your lifetime. I joined Redfin in 2014 to pursue this passion relentlessly. I like my customers to think of me as their coach throughout the entire process.
